LIBRARY TRACKER

WEBSITE

Designed web application for WCS (Women in Computer Science) Officers and members to be able to keep track of the books in the office library. To be used as a tool to display the variety of books in the library, keep track of borrowed and returned book, allow members to reserve and request books, and to mass email overdue notices.
